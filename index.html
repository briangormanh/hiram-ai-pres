The Path Forward
1
Publish more content, faster — shrink the time between idea and published piece. Every post that ranks also feeds AI visibility.
2
Aggressive PR outreach — earned media builds the backlinks and brand signals that move rankings. More pitches, more coverage, more authority.
3
Lean into Youtube — the second-largest search engine, owned by Google. Video converts exceptionally well because it’s human-to-human, and every video can be repurposed into blog posts, social clips, and email content.
4
Stabilize the site foundation — avoid unnecessary URL changes that erode earned equity
5
Build authority through subject matter expertise — expert-sourced content, backlinks, trust signals, E-E-A-T
